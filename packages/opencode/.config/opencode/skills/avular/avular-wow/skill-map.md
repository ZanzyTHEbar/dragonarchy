# Skill Map

Use this map when more than one Avular process could apply.

## `avular-design-docs`

Use for:

- medium or large changes
- architecture decisions
- design reviews
- requirement-to-design mapping
- interface changes
- verification planning

Do not use for:

- small, local, low-risk edits with obvious behavior

## `avular-doc-routing`

Use for:

- deciding whether an artifact belongs in the repo, Jira or Confluence, PLM, or SharePoint
- deciding how to link engineering artifacts across systems

Do not use for:

- designing the technical content of the artifact

## `avular-lifecycle-gates`

Use for:

- stage-gate readiness
- determining stage evidence
- identifying missing deliverables
- deciding whether a waiver or escalation is needed

Do not use for:

- ordinary implementation tasks with no stage transition impact

## `avular-product-versioning`

Use for:

- tier language
- version naming
- compatibility boundaries
- release and architecture language involving Tier-1, Tier-2, or Tier-3 products

Do not use for:

- general semantic-version explanations unrelated to Avular product language
